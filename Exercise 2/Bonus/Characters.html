Here are some of the main characters in the series: Invincible

  • Mark Grayson / Invincible: The protagonist of the series, Mark is a high school student who discovers his superpowers and takes on the mantle of Invincible.
  • Omni Man: Mark's father and the most powerful superhero on Earth. He has a mysterious past and plays a significant role in the series.
  • Debbie Grayson: Mark's mother, who is unaware of her husband's true nature and struggles to protect her family.
  • Atom Eve: A fellow superhero and Mark's love interest, who has the ability to manipulate matter.
  • Allen: A Unopan alien from the Coalition of planets bred to be the ultimate warrior.
  • Thragg: The main antagonist of the series, the most powerful Viltrumite destined to restore Viltrum to its former glory.
