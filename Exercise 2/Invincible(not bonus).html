Invincible

Invincible is an American adult animated superhero television series based on the comic book character of the same name by Robert Kirkman, Cory Walker, and Ryan Ottley. The series was created by Kirkman, who also serves as an executive producer alongside David Alpert, Catherine Winder, Simon Racioppa, and Seth Rogen. The show is produced by Skybound Entertainment and Amazon Studios.

The series follows Mark Grayson, a teenager who inherits superpowers from his father, Omni Man, who is the most powerful superhero on Earth. As Mark learns to control his abilities and navigate the challenges of being a superhero, he discovers dark secrets about his father's past and the true nature of his powers.

Overview

Invincible is a gripping and action-packed series that explores themes of family, identity, and the consequences of power. The show has received critical acclaim for its storytelling, character development, and animation style. It has been praised for its mature themes and complex characters, making it a standout in the superhero genre.

Characters

  • Mark Grayson / Invincible: The protagonist of the series, Mark is a teenager who inherits superpowers from his father. He struggles to balance his normal life with his responsibilities as a superhero.
  • Nolan Grayson / Omni-man: Mark's father and the most powerful superhero on Earth. He has a complex relationship with his son and hides dark secrets about his past.
  • Debbie Grayson: Mark's mother, who is unaware of her husband's true nature and struggles to protect her family.
  • Atom Eve: A fellow superhero and Mark's love interest. She has the ability to manipulate matter and is a key ally in Mark's journey as a superhero.
